Josh's Secret Family Recipie

The Eggroll (Lumpia)

An Old family recipie my mother brought back from the Philippines. The most popular Hors d'oeuvre at partys and a holiday favorite.

Things You Will Need

  • TYJ Spring Roll Pastry
  • 1 Pound Ground Pork
  • 5 Carrots
  • 1 Can Water Chestnuts
  • Salt, Black Pepper, Garlic Powder
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1L Cooking Oil
  • Large Cooking Pot
  • Large Mixing Bowl

How To Make Filling

  1. Finely dice carrots and chestnuts.
  2. Hand mix carrots, chestnuts and ground beef in large mixing bowl.
  3. Add 1 tbsp salt, 1tbsp Black pepper and 1tbps Garlic Powder
  4. Hand work mixture

Rolling Eggrolls

Easy How to Video of Rolling Eggrolls

  1. Use 2 eggs to create eggwash
  2. Layout square wanton wrapper
  3. Evenly distribute 1/3 cup filling at bottom of roll
  4. Roll up eggroll as if rolling a cigar
  5. Use Egg wash to seal Eggroll close
  6. Cut rolled eggrolls into thirds.

Bring 1L cooking oil to 375 degrees. Only fry 6 eggrolls at a time for 5 minutes or untill golden brown. Use tongs to remove cooked eggrolls and let drain in collander.

Serve Hot and Enjoy!!!
